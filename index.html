SUSHIKSHA

Sushiksha- The Flagship Program of T.O.G is in true senses the heart and soul of our organization.

With over 85 students under our umbrella, we not only believe in imparting proper education but the holistic development of the students. We make whole hearted efforts to make sure the students go through properly curated lessons on Mannerism, Hygiene and Sanitation.

Most of our students are below 15 years are getting formal education from government schools but we all know how those schools are. We divide the students into different groups and sections based upon their skills and pre-requisite knowledge to make sure we have alike minds in a certain group,

Sushiksha bridges the gap between the private education and government education standards of our country, currently at a smaller scale.

SLUM-ADOPTION

Mahatma Gandhi once said "Be the change you want to see in the world".

Thus bringing a substantial change in the lives of the people in need, has always been the top motive of Teens of God.Therefore, with this positive approach, we have taken a major step towards our second flagship program, The Slum Adoption Drive, which is based on adopting slums and giving a better life to the underprivileged.

This was initiated in 2019, when our team adopted 20 families, consisting of 80 people of different age groups and hence diverse needs, in Sector-31, Gurugram. During this span of time, Teens of God has done a lot, both physically and emotionally to uplift the lives of these underprivileged people. May it be providing basic living facilities to the people in need or assuring that they’re kept under full sanitation and hygiene, Teens of God has always handled the situation in hand with full managerial skills. We’ve also made them aware of their rights and the government schemes which are meant for them by educating them with all the basic information.

#WhoWeAre

Teens of God is a registered NGO, which works toward helping marginalised communities and towards reducing educational inequalities. This initiative was pioneered by our Founders, Aayush Jain and Prabhpreet Guliani in 2018. We initially started our working by helping small NGO’s with their basic needs. We now have our own education program, we’ve adopted a slum, we conduct various drives(Food drives, Heath camps) also organise workshops for the holistic development of the underprivileged children.
